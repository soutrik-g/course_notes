# Laws and Rules
#### Distribution Laws of Quantifiers
$$\begin{align*}
	\forall x (P(x) \land Q(x)) 
		&\equiv
		\forall x P(x) \land \forall x Q(x)
	\\
	\exists x (P(x) \lor Q(x))
		&\equiv
		\exists x P(x) \lor \exists x Q(x)
\end{align*}$$
